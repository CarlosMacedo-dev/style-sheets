The Wall Photography Style Guide

Overview

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to showcase the work of the photographer of The Wall Photography in order to promote the studio's services and to provide the customers with information about the business.

Target Audience

The website will be primarily focused on woman between 20 and 60 years old that are mostly stay at home mothers and live in a household that averages $65,000 or more yearly income.

Color Palette

I will be using black and white as primary and secondary colors because they are neutral so they will combine well with the photographs no matter what the color of the composition. I will be using a shade of blue and a shade of gray for accents, these are also simple colors that won't drive attention away from the main content.
